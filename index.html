About

I am a self-driven developer who is passionate about bringing fun ideas into life.

I developed casual and story-driven games in 2D and 3D using Unity and shipped them on mobile on the Google Play Store. Since most of my games are self-made, I had to take care of Game Design, Art (drawing), Balancing and Programming. This allowed me to be a good communicator with other fellow developers I worked with, who worked on other development fields such as art and design.

My main interest in development is problem-solving in general and programming (specially gameplay). My main focus when programming a feature is that it works and its code is clean and optimized.

Currently, I am learning Unreal Engine 5 to extend my expertise in programming from C# (Unity) to C++ as well.

Research

Bachelor's thesis: "Programmatic Calculations for Adjusting Dynamic Gameplay Difficulty"

In this research, I came up with mathematical formulas that calculate the difficulty of a level in action/adventure games based on the most common gameplay challenges present in that genre such as number of enemies, placement of obstacles, etc. I used the assets from one of my games to implement a dynamic difficulty system with the help of the formulas.
